## Strategy note

A desirable route for the FWL theorem is through projection geometry / Gram-Schmidt style decomposition:

- interpret the column spaces of `X‚āĀ` and `[X‚āĀ X‚āā]` as nested subspaces,
- use annihilator/projection operators to partial out the `X‚āĀ` component,
- then show the coefficient on `X‚āā` equals the OLS coefficient from the residualized regression.
